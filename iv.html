Previous Visits

Kuttiyadi Hydel Power Project

ISTE NITC Students' Chapter organised industrial visit, to Kuttiyadi Hydel Power Project, exclusively for electrical engineering students with registrations crossing 60 on 15.11.2014. The highlight of Power Project is its capacity of 225MW, third largest in its category, under KSEB. The interaction with officers and workers gave a clear and practical understanding of power generation and transmission.

Diesel Power Plant, Nallalam

ISTE NITC Students' Chapter had its second industrial visit in current academic year to Peekay Steels and Diesel Powerplant,Calicut, which is the 2nd largest in its category in asia, on 22.11.2014. Visit had more than 40 registrations from mechanical engineering department and provided an insight to working environment in industry.
